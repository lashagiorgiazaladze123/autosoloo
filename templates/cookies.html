Cookies Policy for GeoSolo

This Cookies Policy explains how GeoSolo ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our car marketplace platform. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our platform.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • Security features and user authentication
  • Session management and login status
  • Form data preservation
  • Basic navigation and website functionality

Legal Basis: Legitimate interest (necessary for website functionality)

Retention: Session cookies (deleted when browser closes) or up to 1 year

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience on our platform by remembering your preferences:

  • Language preferences
  • Location settings
  • Display preferences (theme, layout)
  • Search filters and saved searches
  • Favorite listings

Legal Basis: Consent (you can opt out via browser settings)

Retention: Up to 2 years

2.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Traffic sources and referral information
  • Device and browser information
  • User journey and conversion tracking
  • Error tracking and performance monitoring

Legal Basis: Consent (you can opt out via browser settings)

Retention: Up to 2 years

2.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness:

  • Interest-based advertising
  • Retargeting and remarketing
  • Campaign performance measurement
  • Social media integration
  • Affiliate marketing tracking

Legal Basis: Consent (you can opt out via browser settings)

Retention: Up to 2 years

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies. These include:

3.1 Analytics Services

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and user behavior
  • Mixpanel: Provides detailed user interaction analytics
  • Hotjar: Records user sessions for UX improvement

3.2 Social Media Platforms

  • Facebook Pixel: Tracks conversions and optimizes advertising
  • Twitter Pixel: Measures campaign performance
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag: Tracks professional audience engagement

3.3 Payment Processors

  • Stripe: Secure payment processing
  • PayPal: Alternative payment method

3.4 Content Delivery Networks

  • Cloudflare: Website performance and security
  • Amazon CloudFront: Fast content delivery

4. Cookie Management

4.1 Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive notifications before cookies are set
Our Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner or settings panel, where available.

4.2 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our platform:

  • Essential features may not work properly
  • You may need to re-enter information
  • Some personalization features may be unavailable
  • Analytics and performance monitoring may be limited

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Last updated" date.
