Getting Started

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click the "Register" button in the top navigation. You'll need to provide a valid email address and create a secure password. We recommend using a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.

Is GeoSolo free to use?

Yes! Creating an account and browsing car listings is completely free. We only charge fees for premium features or verified dealer accounts.

How do I verify my account?

After registering, you'll receive a verification email. Click the link in the email to verify your account. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Buying a Car

How do I search for cars?

Use our advanced search filters on the main page. You can filter by make, model, year, price range, mileage, fuel type, and many other criteria. Save your favorite searches for quick access later.

What should I check before buying?

Always inspect the vehicle in person, check the vehicle's history report, verify all documentation, and consider having a trusted mechanic perform a pre-purchase inspection. Never send money without seeing the car first.

How do I contact a seller?

Click on any car listing to view details, then use the contact information provided by the seller. Always communicate through official channels and never share sensitive personal information.

Is my purchase protected?

GeoSolo facilitates connections between buyers and sellers but doesn't directly handle transactions. We recommend using secure payment methods and meeting in public places for inspections.

Selling Your Car

How do I list my car for sale?

Create an account, then click "Sell Your Car" in the navigation. Fill out the detailed form with accurate information about your vehicle, including photos, specifications, and pricing. High-quality photos significantly increase your chances of selling quickly.

How much should I price my car?

Research similar vehicles in your area using our marketplace data. Consider factors like mileage, condition, features, and local market demand. We provide pricing insights to help you set competitive prices.

What information do I need to provide?

Provide complete and accurate details including: make, model, year, mileage, condition, features, price, location, and contact information. The more detailed your listing, the better your chances of attracting serious buyers.

How do I handle inquiries from buyers?

Respond promptly to all inquiries. Be prepared to answer questions about the vehicle's condition, history, and availability. Schedule viewings at convenient times and locations.

Account & Profile

How do I edit my profile?

Go to your Profile page and click "Edit Profile". You can update your personal information, contact details, and account preferences.

How do I change my password?

Visit your Profile page and select "Change Password". You'll need to enter your current password and choose a new secure password.

How do I delete my account?

If you need to delete your account, please contact our support team. We'll help you through the process and ensure all your data is properly removed.

How do I report a problem?

Use the "Contact Us" form or email our support team directly. Include as much detail as possible about the issue you're experiencing.

Safety & Security

How does GeoSolo protect my data?

We use industry-standard encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits to protect your personal information. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

What should I do if I suspect fraud?

Immediately report suspicious activity to our support team. Don't share personal information with unverified users, and never send money without proper verification.

Are transactions secure?

While we don't process payments directly, we recommend using secure payment methods and meeting in safe, public locations for vehicle inspections and transfers.
